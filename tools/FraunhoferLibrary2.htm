Document Test Library

The document test library contains several documents in different document categories that are compliant to the OOXML standards. Most documents have been created with Microsoft Office 2010. Therefore these documents are following the ISO/IEC 29500:2008-Part4 rules (transitional). Most documents include MCE extensions. Because the validation of OOXML documents containing such extensions requires some pre-processing that is not supported by the available validators the validation of these documents will show MCE-related errors or warnings.

Older OOXML documents created with Microsoft Office 2007 or similar tools may be compliant to ECMA-376-1st edition. In the future documents that are compliant to ISO/IEC 29500:2008-Part1 rules (strict) will be available, too.
